About UNTalent.io

Your Gateway to International Organization Careers

UNTalent.io is the premier job search platform for individuals seeking opportunities within International Organizations (IOs), with a particular focus on the United Nations system. Launched in 2024, our mission is to provide job seekers with the most comprehensive and user-friendly experience in finding their ideal position within the international public sector.

What Sets Us Apart

Our platform stands out by offering an extensive database of job listings from across the UN system and other IOs, coupled with powerful filtering capabilities. Users can refine their search based on:

  • Work experience requirements
  • Educational qualifications
  • Language skills
  • Geographical location
  • Specific agencies
  • Job grades and more

This tailored approach allows candidates to find positions that best match their unique profiles and career aspirations in the world of international organizations.

Our Commitment

At UNTalent.io, we're dedicated to:

  1. Aggregating opportunities from a wide range of UN agencies and IOs
  2. Providing an intuitive, efficient search experience
  3. Empowering job seekers with relevant information and tools

Independent Platform

While we strive to offer the most comprehensive listing of IO jobs available, it's important to note that UNTalent.io is not affiliated with or endorsed by any UN agency or International Organization.
